# Troubleshooting Self-Assessment (Mid to Senior)

## Questions
1) Describe your incident workflow from page to resolution.
2) When do you choose rollback vs forward fix?
3) How do you validate that a fix worked?
4) How do you handle an alert storm?
5) What is your approach to diagnosing latency spikes?
6) How do you debug a CrashLoopBackOff?
7) How do you prevent recurrence after an incident?
8) What do you document during an incident?
9) How do you triage dependency failures?
10) How do you handle data integrity during mitigation?
11) How do you isolate a noisy neighbor issue?
12) How do you communicate status to stakeholders?

## Rubric
- 0-4: needs structured workflow and core triage skills.
- 5-8: solid mid-level; improve mitigation safety and validation.
- 9-12: senior-ready; strong incident leadership and prevention.
