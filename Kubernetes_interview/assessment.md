# Kubernetes Self-Assessment (Mid to Senior)

## Questions
1) How do you handle a stuck rollout safely?
2) How do you design readiness and liveness probes?
3) What is your process for Pending pods?
4) How do you prevent node drain outages?
5) How do you troubleshoot DNS failures?
6) How do you protect secrets at scale?
7) How do you do safe config changes?
8) How do you handle stateful upgrades?
9) How do you debug 5xx spikes?
10) How do you validate a rollback?
11) How do you enforce policy in clusters?
12) How do you prevent noisy neighbors?

## Rubric
- 0-4: needs core operational knowledge.
- 5-8: solid mid-level; improve safety controls.
- 9-12: senior-ready; strong reliability and policy design.
