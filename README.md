This repository, **Shakilkhan24/interview**, is a comprehensive collection of resources focused on **DevOps, infrastructure automation, and interview preparation**. It covers a wide range of modern engineering practices, from low-level networking to high-level system design.

# Shakilkhan24/interview

### **Project Overview**
This repository serves as a centralized hub for mastering DevOps tools and preparing for technical interviews. It includes documentation, mastery guides, and specific interview questions across various domains.

### **Repository Structure**
The repository is organized into specialized folders, each targeting a specific area of the DevOps ecosystem:

*   **Automation & Configuration:** `Ansible-Mastery`, `Helm-Mastery`, `Kustomize-Mastery`.
*   **Infrastructure & Orchestration:** `Infrastructure-As-Code-Terraform`, `Kubernetes_interview`, `AWS-CLI`.
*   **Pipeline & Security:** `CICD`, `DevSecOps-Master`.
*   **Cloud Native & Containers:** `CNCF-Tools`, `Containerization-DevOps`.
*   **Development & Scripting:** `Python-DevOps`, `Go-DevOps`, `linux-bash-devops-interview.md`.
*   **Monitoring & Reliability:** `Monitoring-Observability-DevOps`, `troubleshooting`.
*   **Data & Networking:** `Kafka`, `SQL-Mastery`, `Networking-DevOps`.
*   **Interview Prep:** `DevOps-Interview-Questions`, `System-Design`, `DevOps-Diagrams`.

### **Key Features**
*   **Comprehensive Interview Guides:** Specialized resources for Kubernetes and general DevOps roles.
*   **DevOps Masteries:** Deep dives into essential tools like Git, Ansible, and Terraform.
*   **Practical DevOps:** Includes folders for networking, CLI tools, and real-world troubleshooting.
