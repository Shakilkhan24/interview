# Ansible Self-Assessment (Mid to Senior)

## Questions
1) Explain how you would prevent downtime during an Ansible deploy.
2) Describe variable precedence and one real pitfall.
3) When is `include_tasks` preferred over `import_tasks`?
4) Explain how to debug a failing task in CI.
5) What are the risks of using `shell` in Ansible?
6) How would you implement blue/green with Ansible?
7) How do you manage secrets across environments?
8) Explain how handlers work and a common mistake.
9) How do you make playbooks faster on large fleets?
10) How do you validate idempotence?
11) What is the best way to test a role?
12) When should you avoid Ansible?

## Rubric
- 0-4: Needs stronger fundamentals and safe-change thinking.
- 5-8: Solid mid-level; refine rollout and testing practices.
- 9-12: Senior-ready; strong reliability and scale awareness.
