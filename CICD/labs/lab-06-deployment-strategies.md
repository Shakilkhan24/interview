# Lab 06: Deployment Strategies
Difficulty: Hard
Time: 90 minutes

## Objective
- Implement canary and blue/green deploy strategies.

## Prerequisites
- Deployable app and basic CI pipeline

## Steps
1) Deploy canary with 10 percent traffic.
2) Monitor error rate and latency.
3) Promote or rollback based on results.

## Validation
- Successful promotion or rollback based on metrics.

## Cleanup
- Remove extra environments or traffic rules.
