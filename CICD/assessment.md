# CI/CD Self-Assessment (Mid to Senior)

## Questions
1) How do you ensure artifact immutability?
2) What gates are mandatory before prod deploy?
3) How do you implement rollback safely?
4) How do you prevent secrets from leaking?
5) Explain canary vs blue/green tradeoffs.
6) How do you manage multi-region deploys?
7) How do you handle flaky tests?
8) What metrics do you use to validate deploys?
9) How do you secure runners?
10) How do you document release traceability?
11) How do you test pipeline changes?
12) When do you pause a pipeline?

## Rubric
- 0-4: needs core CI/CD fundamentals.
- 5-8: solid mid-level; improve safety and traceability.
- 9-12: senior-ready; strong reliability and governance.
